Le petit Prince

Le petit Prince est un enfant et un adulte qui quitte son petit astéroide pour voyager dans le cosmos. Pendant la promenade, il visite six planètes différentes, habitées par des personnages étranges: un roi, un vaniteux,un ivrogne, un homme d'affaires, un homme qui allume et éteint un lampadaire, un géographe.

Bugs Bunny

He is a tricky, charismatic, and shrewd rabbit. These personality traits are what gives him an advantage over his enemies, rivals, and opponents. He is also known for his famous catchphrase; "Eh, what's up, doc?", which he typically uses as a greeting to anyone he encounters (usually while munching a carrot).
